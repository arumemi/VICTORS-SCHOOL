About us

Rock Star Study Abroad is a student-centric academic organization dedicated to making education abroad accessible to all students. We bring together a global community of higher education institutions committed to promoting shared understanding and cultural awareness through life-changing global experiences.

Our work is driven by the belief that study abroad should be within reach for every student—no matter their background. This commitment was the purpose behind our inception: in 2005, WHITEROCK the parent company was founded with a clear goal—to expand access to international education through multilateral exchange.

For 20 years now, that vision has guided our growth, shaping the inclusive mission we uphold today at Rock Star Study Abroad.

GOAL:

We are dedicated to breaking down barriers associated with studying abroad to make cross-cultural education accessible to everyone.

Diversity:

We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment for students of all backgrounds, and our dynamic global community spanning across continents and over 150 institutions fosters diverse perspectives and the exchange of ideas, knowledge, and people.
